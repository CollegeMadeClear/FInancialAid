Built from both sides of the desk

College Made Clear started with a simple observation: most families approach college admissions with a lot of anxiety and not enough strategy. They fill out FAFSA because someone told them to. They apply to schools without understanding what the financial aid offer will actually mean. They build lists without a framework.

I built this to fix that. Seven years of college counseling, an institutional research background, and deep expertise in California homeschool pathways gave me a specific view of where families get stuck — and what actually moves the needle.

"FAFSA is a form, not a strategy. The strategy comes from understanding how schools award aid, what drives merit scholarships, and how to build a list where the financial outcomes are predictable."

The tools and resources here are built on that framework. Free tools to do the math. Paid products to do the tracking. Video content to explain the concepts. All of it designed to give families clarity and calm — not more noise.
