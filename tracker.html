The five-level system

A smarter way to build your list

Most trackers use Reach / Match / Safety. This one uses five levels so you can see the real shape of your list — and where it needs work.

High Reach
Stats below typical range or <15% acceptance. A genuine long shot.
Reach
At or below the middle 50%. You're a real candidate, but unpredictable.
Target
Solidly in the middle 50%. You're competitive. Build your list here.
Likely
Above the middle 50%. Strong candidate with good odds. Every list needs these.
Highly Likely
Well above the typical profile. Your safety net — pick ones you'd love to attend.
